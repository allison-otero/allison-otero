## Hi, I'm Allison Otero

I am a Biomedical Engineering student at Florida Institute of Technology with a strong interest in systems engineering, AI/ML, embedded systems, biomedical sensing, signal processing, and robotics.

My work focuses on building intelligent, sensor-driven engineering systems that connect hardware, software, data, and human-centered design. I am especially interested in projects that require full-system thinking: defining requirements, integrating sensors and microcontrollers, processing real-world signals, developing machine learning models, testing system performance, and communicating technical results clearly.

I enjoy working on multidisciplinary projects that combine biomedical engineering, systems design, and intelligent technologies. My current focus includes wearable sensing systems, sEMG-based signal analysis, embedded AI/ML, robotics, real-time data visualization, and systems-level project development.
